Important Appointment Information

If you think you are experiencing a medical or psychological emergency, call 911 immediately or proceed to the nearest emergency room! SHCS Acute Care and Crisis Consultation Services are not available through this scheduling system.

If your health concern falls into any of the following categories, students should contact us at 530.752.2349. UC Davis employees should seek care with their Primary Care Provider:

  • Abdominal Pain
  • Difficulty Breathing
  • Chest Pain
  • Possible Stroke
  • Severe Pain
  • Dizziness or Loss of Consciousness
  • Trauma/Accident
  • Urgent Mental Health Concern

COVID Vaccine appointments will be added as vaccine supplies are confirmed. Please DO NOT call Student Health or Occupational Health to schedule a COVID Vaccination appointment. All vaccinations will be scheduled using this system. Check the Campus Ready website for additional information on scheduling eligibility.

Students: If you are unable to schedule an appointment that meets your needs, please phone the Appointment Desk: 530.752.2349 for medical appointments or 530.752.0871 for Counseling Services.
